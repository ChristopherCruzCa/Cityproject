Camprodón.

Gerona, Cataluña, España.

Camprodón (officially and in Catalan Camprodon) is a municipality in the Ripollés region, in the province of Gerona, in the autonomous community of Catalonia in Spain. Located 120 km from Barcelona and 75 km from Gerona, in the valley of the same name, at the confluence of the Ter and Ritort rivers, Camprodón is one of the largest municipalities in the region, thanks to the annexation of Freixanet, Rocabruna and Beget. It has a population of 2214 inhabitants.
